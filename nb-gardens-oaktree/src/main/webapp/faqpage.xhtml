There are two easy ways to find just what you want on NB Gardens - browse our product range or search for specific items.

From anywhere on NB Gardens you can instantly nip back to the homepage. Just click on the NB Gardens logo in the top left corner of your screen.

Browse

It's simple to browse all our online product range - just click on one of our main product categories. Do this from the homepage or with the navigation bar at the top of your screen. Each time you choose a category, the menu on the left-hand side of your screen shows a list of relevant sub-categories and criteria you can select for more browsing.

Search

Search quickly for products by name, brand or size. Just use the search box in the top right corner. Simply enter the kind of product - bed or power drill, for example. Or type in the brand name such as Black and Decker or Flymo. All the products that match your chosen word will be displayed.
